About Thyroid Disease

Thyroid disease is a medical condition affecting the thyroid gland, a crucial part of the endocrine system. The thyroid regulates metabolism, energy production, and various bodily functions.

Hypothyroidism

Occurs when the thyroid gland doesn't produce enough hormones. Symptoms include fatigue, weight gain, and cold intolerance.

Hyperthyroidism

Characterized by an overactive thyroid, leading to symptoms such as weight loss, rapid heartbeat, and anxiety.

Hashimoto Disease

An autoimmune condition causing inflammation of the thyroid. It often leads to hypothyroidism.

Precautionary Methods

  • Regular thyroid check-ups
  • Healthy diet with iodine-rich foods
  • Manage stress through relaxation techniques
  • Stay physically active
  • Follow prescribed medications
